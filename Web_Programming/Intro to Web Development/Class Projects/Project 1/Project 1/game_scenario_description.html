My Opinion of Modern Warefare 2 Scenarios!

My Favorite Scenarios
  1. Sabotage
  2. Capture The Flag
  3. Domination
  4. Demolition

Reasons why Sabotage is Awesome!

  1. Sabotage is good because there is 20 minutes of free killing.
  2. You can get a lot more kills in sabotage than any other scenarios.
  3. Also, you can get a lot more killstreaks in it too.
  4. Each kill is worth 100 points instead of 50 points.
  5. If you blow the bomb up at a specific time within the 20 minutes game then you can achieve around 10,000 points.

Some downfalls of Sabotage Can Be!

  • Some people decide to try to blow up the bomb at the beginning of the game.
  • There are people who will just continuously try to plant the bomb, just so they can get more experience points.
  • If you lose in overtime then it ends up in a tie.
  • If you have random people on your team and they don't have mic's then there is a possibility that you won't win unless you can get them to cooperate.
