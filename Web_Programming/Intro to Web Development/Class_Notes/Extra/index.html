I am going to tell you some information about MW2 and my preference of Modern Warefare 2. Call of Duty Modern Warefare 2 is a multiplayer game. This game is also a first-person shooter game. On some of the game scenarios you can play from third person view but only in those game scenarios.

Main Menu Game Options

  • Special Ops
  • Campaign
  • Multiplayer

The three game options that are displayed above are the same three game options that are displayed at the main menu of Call of Duty Modern Warefare 2. Special Ops is where you completes missions. There are three different difficulties and they are: Regular, Hardened, Veteran. Each level you can get another star. You can earn up to a maximum amount of three stars for each mission. There is a total of 69 stars for Special Ops.

Description of the Online Gaming Scenarios

  • Team Deathmatch - is where the first team to get to 7500 wins. Also, there is a 10 minute time limit and each kill is 100 points and the assists are 20 points.
  • Mercenary Team Death Match - is where the rules are like Team Deathmatch except there are no parties allowed and with random team mates. The score limit is 7500 and the time limit goes to 10 minutes. Each kill is 100 points and the assists are 20 points.
  • Hardcore Mosh Pit - is a mixed bag of many hardcore game modes in one playlist.
