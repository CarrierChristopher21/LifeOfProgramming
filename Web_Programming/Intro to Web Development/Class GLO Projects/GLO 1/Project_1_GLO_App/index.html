Ways to stay safe and enjoy yourself!

  1. Men's Soccer Game
    • Always wear protective gear if you are playing
    • If you are watching try to consider the people around you.
    • Always make sure you have a good snack to munch on while watching the game!
  2. Firework's Display
    • Always stay behind the area that is off limits to stay safe!
    • Bring a blanket if you want to watch it while sitting on the grass.
    • Arrive early so you can find a good spot to watch the show from a good distance.
  3. Spirit Bonfire
    • Don't get to close to the fire.
    • Be careful while putting the firewood in the fire.
    • Join in the fun when you start seeing people dance.
  4. Alumni & Athletic Hall of Fame Dinner
    • Try to use manners
    • If there's alcohol don't get intoxicated to the point you are a nuisance
    • Also Make sure you enjoy yourself!
    • Stuff yourself till you feel like your going to explode.
  5. Madden Tournament
    • Always play honourably
    • Always be fair and play clean.
    • Play with honest sportsmanship even when you lose.
