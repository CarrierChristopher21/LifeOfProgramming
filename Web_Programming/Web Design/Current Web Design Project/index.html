Description of the Online Gaming Scenarios
  • Team Deathmatch - is where the first team to get to 7500 wins. Also, there is a 10 minute time limit and each kill is 100 points and the assists are 20 points.
  • Mercenary Team Death Match - is where the rules are like Team Deathmatch except there are no parties allowed and with random team mates. The score limit is 7500 and the time limit goes to 10 minutes. Each kill is 100 points and the assists are 20 points.
  • Free-For-All - is about where everyone is versing everyone else and there are no team-mates. The time limit is 10 minutes long and the score limit to reach is 1500 points and each kill is 50 points and the assists are 10 points.
  • Domination - is where your team has to hold three bases or at least hold two bases most of the game so that the first one to 200 points wins. The kills are worth 50 points and the assists are worth 20 points.
  • Ground War - is about Team Deathmatch and Domination. The Team Deathmatch score goes to 10,000 points and each kill is worth 50 points and assists are worth 20 points. The time limit for Team Deathmatch is 10 minutes long. The Domination score goes up to 200 and no time-limit. The main objective is to capture and hold all three flags.
  • Demolition - is about destroying the objects if on offence, or defend the objective(s) if on defense. The game starts with 3 minutes on the clock. The offensive objective is to destroy targets A and B, where as the defense is trying to keep the targets in tact. To destroy a target, the offensive team needs to plant the bomb, which ever member holds and is unstoppable, at both targets, and have them blow up. Planting the bomb takes 5 seconds, the bomb timer lasts 45 seconds, and the defuse time is 5 seconds. If a target is destroyed, 3 minutes are added onto the second round.
  • Sabotage - is where there is one bomb which is usually located in the center of the map, two targets for each team. Each team has to destroy the other target. The planting time is 5 seconds while the defusing time is 5 seconds and the timer lasts for 45 seconds. There is two rounds; the normal round is 20 minutes long and have a 8 second respond time; but if you don't blow up the opponents target then you have overtime which is just like Search and Destroy. The overtime time limit lasts 2-3 minutes.
  • Headquarters Pro - is where you need to capture the headquarters and defend it from the enemy. Headquarters is a respawn mode in which your team must capture HQ points before the enemy team can. Once the game randomly selects the headquarters position, you and your team must get to it as quickly as possible and gain control of it. Take control of the headquarters by standing in the area of the headquarters until the progress bar appears. When the bar reaches full it means that you have successfully taken control of the headquarter point. Now you must defend it once you have secured the headquarters. Once you secure an headquarter, your team can no longer respawn. After the enemy destroys the headquarters, your team regains the ability to respawn. The Pro addition stands for when the headquarters has been randomly selected by the game, it will not be become online (to capture) right away. Instead, both teams have time to get from one side of the map to the position of the next headquarter before it comes online.
  • Search and Destroy - is about where you have one life, so if you die your out for the round; oh yea you have to win four rounds to win the game. There are two bombs. One round, one team protect the bombs and the other team plants, and the next round its vice versa.
  • Barebones Pro - is where you play Team Deathmatch and Free-For-All. The killstreaks are disabled.
  • Team Tactical - is where there is a small based games of Team Deathmatch, Domination, Capture the Flag, Demolition, and Search and Destroy.
  • Hardcore Ricochet:SD - is where it is Hardcore Search and Destroy with richochet friendly fire. It also has Limited HUD, Extra bullet damage, and friendly fire is directed back at the shooter.
  • Hardcore Team Deathmatch - is where it has a limited HUD, Extra bullet damage, and Friendly Fire is on. The health count is in miniscule
  • 3rd Person Team Tactical - Third person prospective with the game scenarios of Team Deathmatch, Domination, Capture the Flag, and Demolition.
  • Team Deathmatch Express - is with a shorter time but I heard its still ten minutes. The score is the same as Team Deathmatch.
  • Objective Barebones Pro - is where you are playing Domination, Demolition, Headquarters Pro, and Capture the Flag. All Killstreaks rewards are disabled.
  • Hardcore Ricochet: HQ Pro - is where it is Headquarter Pro with hardcore Richochet rules.
  • Capture the Flag - There are three rounds. There are two main rounds; each round is five minutes long except overtime. There are two flags each team has there own. You have to return the opponents flag back to your flag to score a point. There is no score limit. Overtime has 3 minutes long. In Overtime the first one to get to the other teams flag wins; no return just grab in win. Each kill is worth 50 points and assists are worth 20 points.
  • Hardcore Mosh Pit - is a mixed bag of many hardcore game modes in one playlist.
