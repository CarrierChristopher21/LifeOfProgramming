This page is about the different types of guns. It will also include the names of the guns. The two type of guns are Primary and Secondary Guns. I will tell you what levels they are unlocked by. The primary guns that you can choose from are: Assault Riffle's, Sub Machine Gun, Light Machine Gun, Sniper Rifle, and the Riot Shield. The Secondary Guns that you can choose from are: Machine Pistols, Shotguns, Handguns, and Launchers.
