This page is about the different types of Gun Attachments and Equipments. It will also include the names of the Gun Attachments and the Equipments. I will tell you the levels of when the equipment's become useable. There are two types of equipment's there is the regular Equipments and there is the Special Grenades. I will also tell you when and how each attachments can become unlocked. The guns that have attachments are: Assault Riffle's, Sub Machine Guns, Light Machine Guns, Sniper Rifles, Machine Pistols, Handguns, and Shotguns.

Primary Gun Attachments

Assault Riffle Attachments

  • No Attachment
  • Grenade Launcher - 10 Kills
  • Red Dot Sight - 25 Kills
  • Silencer - 75 Kills
  • ACOG Scope - 150 Kills
  • FMJ - 300 Kills
  • Shotgun - 20 Kills with Grenade Launcher
  • Holographic Sight - 60 Kills with Red Dot Sight
  • Heartbeat Sensor - 15 Kills with Silencer
  • Thermal Scope - 20 Kills with ACOG Scope
  • Extended Mags - 40 Kills with FMJ

Sub Machine Gun Attachments

  • No Attachment
  • Rapid Fire - 10 Kills
  • Red Dot Sight - 25 Kills
  • Silencer - 75 Kills
  • ACOG Scope - 150 Kills
  • FMJ - 300 Kills
  • Akimbo - 20 Kills with Rapid Fire
  • Holographic Sight - 60 Kills with Red Dot Sight
  • Thermal Scope - 20 Kills with ACOG Scope
  • Extended Mags - 40 Kills with FMJ

Light Machine Gun Attachments

  • No Attachment
  • Grip - 10 Kills
  • Red Dot Sight - 25 Kills
  • Silencer - 75 Kills
  • ACOG Scope - 150 Kills
  • FMJ - 300 Kills
  • Holographic Sight - 60 Kills with Red Dot Sight
  • Heartbeat Sensor - 15 Kills with Silencer
  • Thermal Scope - 20 Kills with ACOG Scope
  • Extended Mags - 40 Kills with FMJ

Sniper Rifles Attachments

  • Silencer - 10 Kills
  • ACOG - 25 Kills
  • FMJ - 75 Kills
  • Heartbeat Sensor - 15 Kills with Silencer
  • Thermal Scope - 20 Kills with ACOG Scope
  • Extended Mags - 40 Kills with FMJ

Secondary Attachments

Machine Pistol Attachments

  • Red Dot Sight - 10 Kills
  • Silencer - 25 Kills
  • FMJ - 50 Kills
  • Akimbo - 75 Kills
  • Holographic Sight - 100 Kills
  • Extended Mags - 150 Kills

Shotgun Attachments

  • No Attachment
  • Red Dot Sight- 10 Kills, Not Available for Ranger or Model 1887
  • Silencer - 25 Kills, Not Available for Ranger or Model 1887
  • Grip - 75 Kills, Not Available for Ranger or Model 1887
  • FMJ - 150 Kills, Available at 50 Kills for Ranger and Model 1887
  • Holographic Sight - 250 Kills, Not Available for Ranger or Model 1887
  • Extended Mags - 400 Kills, Not Available for Ranger or Model 1887
  • Akimbo - 10 Kills, Available for only Ranger and Model 1887

Handgun Attachments

  • No Attachment
  • FMJ - 10 Kills
  • Silencer - 25 Kills, Not Available for Magnum
  • Akimbo - 50 Kills
  • Tactical Knife - 75 Kills, Magnum Needs - 100 Kills
  • Extended Mags - 40 Kills with FMJ, Not Available for Magnum

Machine Pistol Attachments

  • No Attachment for Launchers
  • The Stinger (x 2 Rounds, Most Recommended), Javelin (x 1 Round) and At4-HS (x 1 Round), RPG (x 2 Rounds) can all shoot down aircraft's.
  • The explosive Launchers are At4-HS (x 1 Round), Thumper (x 2 Rounds), Javelin (x 1 Round), and the great RPG (x 2 Rounds).
